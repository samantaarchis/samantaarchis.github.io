Tourist Destinations

  • Haridwar - A holy city on the Ganges, known for Kumbh Mela.
  • Rishikesh - The yoga capital with adventure sports and ashrams.
  • Dehradun - The capital city with forests and educational institutions.
  • Nainital - A lake city with scenic views and boating.

Nature Tourism

  • Jim Corbett National Park - India's oldest national park with tigers and elephants.
  • Valley of Flowers - A UNESCO site with alpine flowers and meadows.
  • Mussoorie - A hill station with colonial architecture and views.
  • Nanda Devi National Park - Home to rare flora and fauna.
  • Auli - Known for skiing and cable car rides.

Religious Sites

  • Kedarnath - One of the 12 Jyotirlingas, part of Chota Char Dham.
  • Badrinath - A temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu.
  • Gangotri - The origin of the Ganges River.
  • Yamunotri - The source of the Yamuna River.
  • Hemkund Sahib - A Sikh gurudwara with a sacred lake.
