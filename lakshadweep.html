Tourist Destinations

  • Kavaratti - The capital island with administrative offices and markets.
  • Agatti - Known for its airport and beautiful beaches.
  • Minicoy - The southernmost island with unique culture and lagoons.
  • Kadmat - Famous for its coconut plantations and marine life.
  • Amini - Offers diving, snorkeling, and water sports.

Nature Tourism

  • Bangaram Island - A coral atoll with lagoons and marine biodiversity.
  • Agatti Beach - Pristine sandy shores ideal for relaxation.
  • Minicoy Lagoon - Known for its clear waters and aquatic life.
  • Kavaratti Reef - Offers snorkeling and diving opportunities.
  • Bitra Island - A secluded spot for eco-tourism and birdwatching.

Religious Sites

  • Ujra Mosque - An ancient mosque in Kavaratti with historical significance.
  • Juma Mosque - A central mosque in Agatti for community prayers.
  • Minicoy Mosque - Reflects the Islamic heritage of the island.
  • Kadmat Mosque - A place of worship with traditional architecture.
  • Amini Mosque - Serves the local Muslim community.
