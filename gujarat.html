Tourist Destinations

  • Statue of Unity: The world's tallest statue, dedicated to Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel.
  • Rani Ki Vav: A UNESCO World Heritage Site, an ancient stepwell with intricate carvings.
  • Somnath Temple: One of the twelve Jyotirlinga shrines, located on the coast.
  • Dwarkadhish Temple: A major Hindu pilgrimage site in Dwarka.
  • Gir National Park: Home to the Asiatic lion, the only place in the world where they are found in the wild.
  • Modhera Sun Temple: A 11th-century temple dedicated to the sun god.
  • Saputara: A hill station known for its scenic beauty and waterfalls.

Nature Tourism

Wildlife

Gir National Park and Sanctuary, Marine National Park in the Gulf of Kutch, Vansda National Park, Nalsarovar Bird Sanctuary, Kutch Bustard Sanctuary, Purna Wildlife Sanctuary, and Blackbuck National Park Velavadar. Gujarat is home to Asiatic lions, wild ass, blackbuck, and migratory birds.

Hills and Valleys

Saputara, Wilson Hills, and Pavagadh Hill offer scenic views and cooler climates.

Lakes and Fields

Nalsarovar Bird Sanctuary is a large wetland attracting flamingos and other birds.

Religious and Pilgrimage Sites

  • Somnath Temple: Rebuilt several times, a sacred Shiva temple.
  • Dwarkadhish Temple: Dedicated to Krishna, believed to be the site of his kingdom.
  • Palitana Temples: Over 900 Jain temples on Shatrunjaya Hill.
  • Ambaji Temple: One of the 51 Shakti Peethas.
  • Hutheesing Jain Temple: A beautiful Jain temple in Ahmedabad.
  • Girnar: A sacred hill with temples and Jain shrines.
