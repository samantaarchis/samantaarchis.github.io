Tourist Destinations

  • Puducherry Town - Known for its French architecture, promenades, and markets.
  • Auroville - An experimental township promoting peace and sustainable living.
  • Chennai - Nearby city with historical sites and modern attractions.
  • Villupuram - Gateway to Puducherry with temples and forts.
  • Cuddalore - Coastal town with beaches and historical forts.

Nature Tourism

  • Botanical Garden - Features exotic plants and a serene environment.
  • Oussudu Bird Sanctuary - A haven for migratory birds and nature lovers.
  • Bharathi Park - A park with statues, fountains, and recreational areas.
  • Paradise Beach - Offers water sports and scenic views.
  • Chunnambar River - Known for boating and picnic spots.

Religious Sites

  • Sri Aurobindo Ashram - A spiritual center founded by Sri Aurobindo.
  • Notre Dame des Anges - A beautiful church with Gothic architecture.
  • Manakula Vinayagar Temple - A Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha.
  • Immaculate Conception Cathedral - A prominent Roman Catholic church.
  • Jama Masjid - A mosque reflecting the Islamic heritage.
