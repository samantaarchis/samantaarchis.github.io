Tourist Destinations

  • Tawang: Famous for Tawang Monastery, the largest monastery in India, and stunning landscapes.
  • Bomdila: A hill station with beautiful views, apple orchards, and the Bomdila Monastery.
  • Ziro: Known for its picturesque valley, rice fields, and the Apatani tribal culture.
  • Itanagar: The capital city with the Ita Fort and Jawaharlal Nehru Museum.
  • Pasighat: Gateway to the Siang Valley with rich tribal heritage.

Nature Tourism

National Parks and Wildlife

Namdapha National Park, the largest protected area in the Eastern Himalaya. Mouling National Park and Pakke Tiger Reserve.

Hills and Valleys

Sela Pass, a high-altitude mountain pass. Ziro Valley with terraced rice fields and pine forests.

Lakes

Ganga Lake, a natural lake surrounded by forests. Madhuri Lake near Pasighat.

Religious and Pilgrimage Sites

  • Tawang Monastery: The largest monastery in India, a major Tibetan Buddhist site.
  • Urgelling Monastery: Birthplace of the 6th Dalai Lama.
  • Dirang Monastery: A historic monastery with beautiful architecture.
