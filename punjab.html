Tourist Destinations

  • Amritsar - Home to the Golden Temple.
  • Chandigarh - A modern planned city.
  • Ludhiana - Known for industrial heritage.
  • Jalandhar - Famous for sports goods.

Nature Tourism

  • Harike Wetland - A Ramsar site with bird watching.
  • Kanjli Wetland - Known for migratory birds.
  • Pong Dam Lake - A reservoir with boating.
  • Sukhna Lake - An artificial lake in Chandigarh.

Religious Sites

  • Golden Temple - The holiest Sikh shrine.
  • Durgiana Temple - A Hindu temple in Amritsar.
  • Hindu Sabha Temple - Another temple in Amritsar.
  • Gobindgarh Fort - A historical fort.
