Tourist Destinations

  • Kochi - The commercial capital with historical forts, Chinese fishing nets, and colonial architecture.
  • Thiruvananthapuram - The capital city known for its temples, museums, and beaches.
  • Kozhikode - Famous for its spice markets, Dutch Cemetery, and cultural heritage.
  • Alappuzha - Known for its backwaters, houseboat cruises, and coir industry.
  • Kollam - Gateway to the backwaters with Chinese fishing nets and historical significance.

Nature Tourism

  • Kovalam Beach - One of the most popular beaches in India with palm-fringed shores.
  • Munnar - A hill station famous for tea plantations, wildlife, and misty mountains.
  • Silent Valley National Park - A biodiversity hotspot with rare flora and fauna.
  • Athirappilly Falls - One of the largest waterfalls in Kerala, surrounded by forests.
  • Vembanad Lake - The largest lake in Kerala, ideal for backwater cruises and birdwatching.

Religious Sites

  • Guruvayur Temple - A famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Krishna.
  • Sabarimala - A major pilgrimage site for Lord Ayyappa devotees.
  • Padmanabhaswamy Temple - An ancient temple in Thiruvananthapuram with rich treasures.
  • Malayatoor - A hilltop church known for its annual festival and scenic views.
  • Paradesi Synagogue - One of the oldest synagogues in the Commonwealth, in Kochi.
