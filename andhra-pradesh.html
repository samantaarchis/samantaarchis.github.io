Tourist Destinations

  • Visakhapatnam: Features Kailasagiri park, Visakha Museum, Indira Gandhi Zoological Park, submarine museum, and beaches like RK Beach, Yarada Beach, Rushikonda Beach.
  • Baruva: A beach village with the Mahendratanaya River confluence, lighthouse, and temples.
  • Kalingapatnam: Historic beach with lighthouse and Vamsadhara River confluence.
  • Mandasa: Known for Vasudeva Temple and Mahendragiri Hills.
  • Vijayawada: Prakasam Barrage, Kondapalli Fort, Bhavani Island.
  • Rajahmundry: On the banks of Godavari River.
  • Talakona: Scenic waterfall in Tirupati district.

Nature Tourism

Beaches

Visakhapatnam has several beaches along the Bay of Bengal. Other notable beaches include Mypadu and Suryalanka.

Caves

Borra Caves in Ananthagiri Hills with stalactites and stalagmites. Belum Caves, the second largest natural caves in India.

Hills and Valleys

Araku Valley with forests, coffee plantations, and waterfalls. Horsley Hills, a summer resort. Papi Hills near Bhadrachalam.

Lakes and Fields

Kolleru Lake, a wildlife sanctuary. Pulicat Lake. Konaseema with paddy fields and coconut groves.

Religious and Pilgrimage Sites

  • Tirumala Venkateswara Temple: One of the richest temples in the world, abode of Venkateswara.
  • Srisailam: Mallikarjuna Swamy temple, one of the twelve Jyotirlinga shrines.
  • Kanaka Durga Temple: In Vijayawada, famous for Dusshera celebrations.
  • Simhachalam: Abode of Narasimha, near Visakhapatnam.
  • Pancharama Kshetras: Five ancient Shiva temples at Amararama, Draksharama, etc.
  • Buddhist Sites: Amaravati, Nagarjuna Konda, Bhattiprolu, and others.
