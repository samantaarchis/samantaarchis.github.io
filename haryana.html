Tourist Destinations

  • Kurukshetra: The land of the Mahabharata, where the Bhagavad Gita was delivered.
  • Mansa Devi Temple: A popular temple dedicated to Goddess Mansa, known for its ropeway.
  • Sultanpur National Park: A bird sanctuary and wildlife reserve near Delhi.
  • Suraj Kund: An ancient reservoir with hot springs and historical significance.
  • Pinjore Gardens: Mughal-era gardens with fountains and pavilions.
  • Sohna Hot Springs: Natural hot springs believed to have healing properties.

Nature Tourism

Wildlife

Sultanpur National Park, Kalesar National Park, and Bhindawas Wildlife Sanctuary offer opportunities to see birds, deer, and other wildlife.

Lakes and Fields

Badkhal Lake, Damdama Lake, and Tilyar Lake are popular for picnics and boating.

Hills and Valleys

Morni Hills provide scenic views and trekking opportunities.

Religious and Pilgrimage Sites

  • Kurukshetra: Jyotisar, the birthplace of the Bhagavad Gita.
  • Mansa Devi Temple: A Shakti Peeth and major pilgrimage site.
  • Adi Badri: The origin point of the Saraswati River, with ancient temples.
  • Sheikh Chilli's Tomb: A Sufi shrine in Kurukshetra.
  • Chaneti Buddhist Stupa: An ancient Buddhist site.
