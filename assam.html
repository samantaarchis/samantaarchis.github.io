Tourist Destinations

  • Kaziranga National Park - Home to the Indian rhinoceros and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Manas National Park - A wildlife sanctuary and UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Dibru-Saikhowa National Park - Known for feral horses and biodiversity.
  • Majuli Island - The world's largest river island, famous for Vaishnavite monasteries.
  • Guwahati - Gateway to Northeast India, with Kamakhya Temple.
  • Sivasagar - Historic capital with Rang Ghar and Kareng Ghar.
  • Charaideo - Site of Frang-Mai-Dam, ancient Ahom burial mounds.

Nature Tourism

  • Kaziranga National Park - Wildlife safaris, rhino sightings, and birdwatching.
  • Manas National Park - Tiger reserve and elephant habitats.
  • Dibru-Saikhowa National Park - Feral horses and river cruises.
  • Orang National Park - Elephant reserve.
  • Nameri National Park - Rafting and trekking in the foothills.
  • Assam Tea Gardens - Scenic plantations and tea tours.

Religious Sites

  • Kamakhya Temple - Sacred Shakti Peetha in Guwahati.
  • Majuli Island - Satras (Vaishnavite monasteries) and cultural heritage.
  • Charaideo Maidams - Ancient Ahom royal burial grounds.
  • Basistha Temple - Ancient temple dedicated to sage Basistha.
