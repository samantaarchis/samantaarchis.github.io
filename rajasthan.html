Tourist Destinations

  • Jaipur - The Pink City with palaces and forts.
  • Udaipur - The City of Lakes with palaces.
  • Jodhpur - Known as the Blue City.
  • Jaisalmer - The Golden City in the desert.

Nature Tourism

  • Ranthambore National Park - Famous for tigers.
  • Sariska Tiger Reserve - A wildlife sanctuary.
  • Keoladeo National Park - A bird sanctuary.
  • Desert National Park - Sand dunes and wildlife.

Religious Sites

  • Eklingji Temple - Dedicated to Lord Shiva.
  • Dilwara Temples - Jain temples in Mount Abu.
  • Birla Temple - A Hindu temple in Jaipur.
  • Amber Fort - A historical fort with temples.
