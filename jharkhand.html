Tourist Destinations

  • Netarhat: A serene hill station known for its pine forests and waterfalls.
  • Betla National Park: Home to tigers, elephants, and various wildlife species.
  • Hundru Falls: One of the highest waterfalls in Jharkhand, surrounded by forests.
  • Baidyanath Temple: One of the twelve Jyotirlinga shrines, a major Hindu pilgrimage site.
  • Parasnath: The highest peak in Jharkhand, a Jain pilgrimage site.
  • Ranchi: The capital city with lakes, museums, and cultural sites.

Nature Tourism

Wildlife

Betla National Park, Hazaribag Wildlife Sanctuary, Dalma Wildlife Sanctuary, Koderma Wildlife Sanctuary, Palkot Wildlife Sanctuary, and Mahuadanr Wolf Sanctuary.

Hills and Valleys

Netarhat hill station offers scenic views and trekking opportunities.

Lakes and Fields

Dimna Lake, Hazaribagh Jheel, and Raja Talab are popular for boating and picnics.

Waterfalls

Hundru Falls, Jonha Falls, Dassam Falls, Lodh Falls, and Usri Falls.

Religious and Pilgrimage Sites

  • Baidyanath Temple: A sacred Shiva temple in Deoghar.
  • Parasnath: A Jain tirthankara site with temples and stupas.
  • Rajrappa: Chhinnamasta Temple on the confluence of rivers.
  • Jagannath Temple: A replica of the Puri temple in Ranchi.
  • Dewri Temple: An ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva.
  • Kapilnath Temple: A historical Shiva temple.
