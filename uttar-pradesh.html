Tourist Destinations

  • Agra - Home to the Taj Mahal and Mughal architecture.
  • Lucknow - Known as the city of Nawabs with palaces and cuisine.
  • Varanasi - The spiritual capital with ghats and temples.
  • Prayagraj - The city of confluence with Kumbh Mela.

Nature Tourism

  • Dudhwa National Park - A tiger reserve with grasslands and wetlands.
  • Chambal River - Known for river safaris and gharials.
  • Okhla Bird Sanctuary - A haven for migratory birds.
  • Soor Sarovar Bird Sanctuary - Famous for birdwatching.
  • Pilibhit Tiger Reserve - Home to tigers and swamp deer.

Religious Sites

  • Kashi Vishwanath Temple - A sacred temple in Varanasi.
  • Mathura-Vrindavan - Birthplace of Lord Krishna.
  • Ayodhya - Birthplace of Lord Rama.
  • Prayagraj - Site of the Kumbh Mela at the confluence of rivers.
  • Naimisaranya - A sacred forest with temples.
