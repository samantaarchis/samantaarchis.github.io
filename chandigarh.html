Tourist Destinations

  • Sector-17 - The commercial hub with markets and plazas.
  • Burail Fort - A historical fort with Mughal architecture.
  • Manimajra Fort - An ancient fort with historical significance.
  • Elante Mall - A modern shopping and entertainment complex.
  • Paras Downtown Square - A lifestyle shopping destination.
  • Tagore Theatre - A cultural venue for performances.

Nature Tourism

  • Rock Garden of Chandigarh - An artistic garden made from recycled materials.
  • Zakir Hussain Rose Garden - A beautiful rose garden with varieties of roses.
  • Japanese Garden - A serene garden with Japanese landscaping.
  • Parrot Bird Sanctuary - A sanctuary for birds and nature lovers.
  • Mahendra Chaudhary Zoological Park - A zoo with various animals.
  • Sukhna Lake - An artificial lake for boating and picnics.
  • Sukhna Wildlife Sanctuary - A sanctuary around the lake.
  • Rose Festival - Annual festival celebrating roses.
  • Heritage Trees of Chandigarh - Notable trees in the city.
  • Sukhna Interpretation Centre - Educational center about the lake.
  • Terraced Garden - A garden with terraced landscapes.
  • Leisure Valley - A park for relaxation and activities.

Religious Sites

  • Mata Basanti Devi Mandir - A temple dedicated to Goddess Shitala.
  • Chandi Mandir - A temple near Chandigarh.
  • Mata Mansa Devi Mandir - A temple for Goddess Mansa Devi.
  • Mata Jayanti Devi Mandir - Another temple in the vicinity.
  • Saketri Shiv Mandir - A temple dedicated to Lord Shiva.
  • Nada Sahib Gurudwara - A Sikh place of worship.
  • Mosques in Manimajra and Burail - Historical mosques.
  • Diocese of Simla and Chandigarh - Catholic cathedral.
