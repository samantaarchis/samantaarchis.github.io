Tourist Destinations

  • Bhopal - The capital city with lakes, museums, and historical monuments.
  • Indore - Known for its palaces, markets, and proximity to hill stations.
  • Jabalpur - Gateway to wildlife areas with marble rocks and waterfalls.
  • Gwalior - Famous for its fort, palaces, and classical music heritage.
  • Ujjain - An ancient city known for its temples and the Kumbh Mela.

Nature Tourism

  • Kanha National Park - Home to tigers, deer, and diverse wildlife.
  • Bandhavgarh National Park - Known for its high tiger density and ancient fort.
  • Pachmarhi - A hill station with waterfalls, caves, and forests.
  • Bhedaghat - Famous for the Marble Rocks and Dhuandhar Falls.
  • Panna National Park - A tiger reserve with Ken River and ancient caves.

Religious Sites

  • Khajuraho Temples - UNESCO World Heritage site with intricate carvings.
  • Sanchi Stupa - Ancient Buddhist monument and UNESCO site.
  • Mahakaleshwar Temple - One of the 12 Jyotirlingas in Ujjain.
  • Omkareshwar Temple - A sacred island temple on the Narmada River.
  • Chanderi - Known for its Jain temples and historical significance.
