Tourist Destinations

  • Port Blair - The capital with historical sites.
  • Havelock Island - Known for beaches and diving.
  • Neil Island - A serene island with lagoons.
  • Diglipur - Gateway to Ross Island.

Nature Tourism

  • Radhanagar Beach - One of Asia's best beaches.
  • Elephant Beach - Famous for snorkeling.
  • Cellular Jail - A national memorial.
  • Viper Island - A former prison island.
  • Mount Harriet National Park - Rainforests and viewpoints.

Religious Sites

  • Mahatma Gandhi Marine National Park - Natural sites with spiritual vibes.
  • Local temples and churches - Reflecting the diverse population.
