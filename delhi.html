Tourist Destinations

  • Red Fort: A UNESCO World Heritage Site and symbol of India's independence.
  • India Gate: A war memorial honoring Indian soldiers.
  • Qutub Minar: The tallest brick minaret in the world.
  • Humayun's Tomb: A Mughal mausoleum and UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Jama Masjid: One of the largest mosques in India.
  • Connaught Place: A commercial hub with shopping and dining.
  • Lotus Temple: A Baháʼí House of Worship shaped like a lotus flower.

Nature Tourism

Wildlife

National Zoological Park, home to various animals and birds.

Parks and Gardens

Garden of Five Senses, Bharat Darshan Park, and Millennium Indraprastha Park.

Religious and Pilgrimage Sites

  • Akshardham Temple: A modern Hindu temple complex with exhibitions and gardens.
  • Laxminarayan Temple: Dedicated to Lakshmi and Narayan.
  • ISKCON Temple: A Krishna temple with beautiful architecture.
  • Gurudwara Bangla Sahib: A prominent Sikh gurdwara.
  • Jama Masjid: A grand mosque built by Shah Jahan.
  • Nizamuddin Dargah: The mausoleum of Sufi saint Nizamuddin Auliya.
  • Lotus Temple: A place of worship for all religions.
