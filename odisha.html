Tourist Destinations

  • Bhubaneswar - The temple city with ancient architecture.
  • Puri - Famous for its beach and Jagannath Temple.
  • Konark - Home to the Sun Temple.
  • Cuttack - Known for silverware and festivals.

Nature Tourism

  • Chilika Lake - Asia's largest brackish water lagoon.
  • Simlipal National Park - A tiger reserve with waterfalls.
  • Chandipur Beach - Known for vanishing tides.
  • Gahirmatha Beach - A marine sanctuary.

Religious Sites

  • Konark Sun Temple - A UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Puri Jagannath Temple - One of the Char Dham.
  • Lingaraj Temple - Dedicated to Lord Shiva.
  • Udayagiri and Khandagiri Caves - Ancient Jain caves.
