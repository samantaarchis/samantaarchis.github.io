Tourist Destinations

  • Raipur - The capital city with modern attractions and historical sites.
  • Bilaspur - Known for its temples and proximity to coal mines.
  • Korba - Industrial hub with nearby waterfalls and forests.
  • Raigarh - Gateway to the Chhattisgarh plains with historical forts.
  • Jagdalpur - Bastar region hub with tribal culture and markets.
  • Ambikapur - Known for its palaces and proximity to wildlife areas.
  • Chirmiri - Coal mining town with nearby national parks.
  • Dhamtari - Rice bowl area with rivers and temples.
  • Mahasamund - Known for its rice fields and ancient temples.
  • Rajnandgaon - Textile hub with historical significance.

Nature Tourism

  • Achanakmar-Amarkantak Biosphere Reserve - UNESCO-recognized reserve with diverse flora and fauna.
  • Kanger Ghati National Park - Known for its caves, waterfalls, and wildlife.
  • Chitrakote Falls - One of the highest waterfalls in India, surrounded by forests.
  • Teerathgarh Falls - Scenic waterfall with religious significance.
  • Maitri Bagh Zoo - Largest zoo in central India with various animal species.
  • Mainpat - Mini Shimla with pleasant climate and hill views.

Religious Sites

  • Danteshwari Temple - Shakti Peetha in Dantewada, dedicated to Goddess Danteshwari.
  • Bhoramdeo Temple - Ancient temple complex with intricate carvings.
  • Sirpur Group of Monuments - Buddhist and Hindu monuments from ancient times.
  • Jogimara Caves - Early Brahmi inscriptions and ancient paintings.
  • Sitabenga Caves - One of the earliest examples of theatre architecture in India.
