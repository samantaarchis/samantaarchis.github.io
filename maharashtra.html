Tourist Destinations

  • Mumbai - The financial capital with Bollywood and beaches.
  • Pune - Known as the cultural capital with forts and gardens.
  • Aurangabad - Gateway to Ajanta and Ellora caves.
  • Nashik - Famous for vineyards and religious sites.

Nature Tourism

  • Lonavala-Khandala - Hill stations with lakes and trekking.
  • Mahabaleshwar - Known for strawberries and viewpoints.
  • Tadoba National Park - A tiger reserve with safaris.
  • Sanjay Gandhi National Park - Urban wildlife in Mumbai.
  • Chikhaldara - A wildlife sanctuary with waterfalls.

Religious Sites

  • Shirdi Sai Baba Temple - A popular pilgrimage site.
  • Pandharpur Vithoba Temple - Dedicated to Lord Vithoba.
  • Ashtavinayaka - Eight temples of Lord Ganesha.
  • Ajanta Caves - UNESCO site with ancient Buddhist art.
  • Ellora Caves - UNESCO site with rock-cut temples.
