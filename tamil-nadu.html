Tourist Destinations

  • Chennai - The capital city with colonial architecture and museums.
  • Madurai - Known for its temples and cultural heritage.
  • Kanyakumari - The southernmost tip with stunning sunrises and sunsets.
  • Rameswaram - An island town famous for its temples and pilgrimage.

Nature Tourism

  • Marina Beach - One of the longest urban beaches in the world.
  • Ooty - A popular hill station with tea plantations and lakes.
  • Mudumalai Tiger Reserve - Home to tigers, elephants, and diverse wildlife.
  • Hogenakkal Falls - Known as the Niagara of India, with boating facilities.
  • Courtallam - Famous for its waterfalls and medicinal baths.

Religious Sites

  • Meenakshi Temple - A historic temple in Madurai dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi.
  • Brihadeeswarar Temple - UNESCO site in Thanjavur with Dravidian architecture.
  • Ramanathaswamy Temple - Located in Rameswaram, one of the Char Dham sites.
  • Vivekananda Rock Memorial - A memorial to Swami Vivekananda off Kanyakumari.
  • Basilica of Our Lady of Good Health - A Catholic church in Velankanni.
