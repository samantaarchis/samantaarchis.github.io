Tourist Destinations

  • Calangute Beach: One of the most popular beaches in Goa, known for its vibrant atmosphere and water sports.
  • Baga Beach: Famous for its nightlife, shacks, and proximity to Calangute.
  • Anjuna Beach: Known for its flea market and hippie culture.
  • Fort Aguada: A 17th-century Portuguese fort with a lighthouse offering panoramic views.
  • Basilica of Bom Jesus: A UNESCO World Heritage Site housing the tomb of St. Francis Xavier.
  • Mangueshi Temple: A Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, known for its architecture.
  • Goa State Museum: Showcases Goa's history, art, and culture.

Nature Tourism

Beaches

Goa is renowned for its stunning beaches along the Arabian Sea. Popular ones include Calangute, Baga, Anjuna, Vagator, and Palolem.

Wildlife

Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary, Bhagwan Mahaveer Sanctuary and Mollem National Park, Cotigao Wildlife Sanctuary, Mhadei Wildlife Sanctuary, and Netravali Wildlife Sanctuary. Dudhsagar Falls is India's fifth tallest waterfall.

Waterfalls

Dudhsagar Falls, Arvalem Falls, Kuske Falls, Kesarval Falls, Tambdi Surla Falls, and Charavane Falls.

Religious and Pilgrimage Sites

  • Basilica of Bom Jesus: UNESCO World Heritage Site with the incorrupt body of St. Francis Xavier.
  • Se Cathedral: The largest church in Asia, dedicated to St. Catherine.
  • Church and Convent of St. Francis of Assisi: Known for its beautiful architecture.
  • Mangueshi Temple: Ancient Shiva temple with intricate carvings.
  • Safa Mosque: One of the oldest mosques in Goa.
  • Mahadev Temple (Tambdi Surla): Rock-cut temple in a cave.
  • Chapel of St. Francis Xavier: Site of the saint's first burial.
