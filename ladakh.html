Tourist Destinations

  • Leh - The capital with palaces, markets, and proximity to monasteries.
  • Western Ladakh - Includes Nubra Valley, Pangong Lake, and Tso Moriri.
  • Central Ladakh - Home to Leh, Hemis, and Thiksey monasteries.
  • Eastern Ladakh - Features Changthang region and nomadic culture.
  • Kargil - Known for its rivers, valleys, and historical significance.

Nature Tourism

  • Pangong Lake - A high-altitude lake with changing colors and migratory birds.
  • Nubra Valley - Famous for sand dunes, double-humped camels, and hot springs.
  • Tso Moriri - A serene lake in the Changthang region.
  • Zanskar Valley - Offers trekking, glaciers, and remote villages.
  • Markha Valley - Known for its trekking routes and natural beauty.

Religious Sites

  • Hemis Monastery - One of the largest and wealthiest monasteries in Ladakh.
  • Thiksey Monastery - Famous for its Maitreya statue and festivals.
  • Alchi Monastery - Known for its ancient murals and Tangyud Monastery.
  • Lamayuru Monastery - Built on a hill with cave dwellings.
  • Diskit Monastery - Features a giant statue of Maitreya Buddha.
