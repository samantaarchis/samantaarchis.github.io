Tourist Destinations

  • Imphal - The capital city with palaces and temples.
  • Kangla Palace - The traditional seat of Manipur rulers.
  • Hiyangthang Lairembi Temple Complex - A religious and tourist site.
  • Ukhrul - A hill station with scenic beauty.
  • Tamenglong - Known for natural beauty and fruit groves.

Nature Tourism

  • Loktak Lake - The world's only floating lake.
  • Thanga Island - The largest island in Loktak Lake.
  • Keibul Lamjao National Park - A floating park for sangai deer.
  • Sirohi National Park - A wildlife sanctuary.
  • Mount Tempü - The highest peak with Dzüko Valley views.
  • Mt. Koubru - A sacred peak for Sanamahi faith.

Religious Sites

  • Jama Masjid - A mosque in Imphal market.
  • Shri Govindajee Temple - A temple with cultural significance.
  • ISKCON Temple - A Krishna temple near the airport.
  • Langol Lairembi Temple - For Sanamahi faith.
  • Langol Ningthou Temple - Another Sanamahi temple.
