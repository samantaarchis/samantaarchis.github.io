Tourist Destinations

  • Jammu - The winter capital with ancient temples and Mughal gardens.
  • Srinagar - Famous for Dal Lake, houseboats, and Mughal architecture.
  • Gulmarg - A ski resort with meadows and cable car rides.
  • Pahalgam - Known for Lidder Valley, Betaab Valley, and trout fishing.
  • Sonmarg - The meadow of gold with glaciers and trekking opportunities.

Nature Tourism

  • Bangus Valley - A scenic valley with pine forests and meadows.
  • Doodhpathri - Known for its meadows and natural springs.
  • Gurez - A remote valley with stunning landscapes and adventure sports.
  • Chenab Valley - Offers rafting, trekking, and scenic views.
  • Verinag - Famous for its spring and Mughal garden.

Religious Sites

  • Bhadrakali Temple - A Hindu temple in Srinagar dedicated to Goddess Kali.
  • Gurudwara Thara Sahib - A Sikh gurudwara marking Guru Gobind Singh's visit.
  • Detha Mandir - An ancient temple in Jammu with historical significance.
  • Kheer Bhawani Temple - A Hindu temple in Tulmul dedicated to Goddess Ragnya.
  • Ancient Stupa Parihaspora - Buddhist ruins showcasing ancient heritage.
